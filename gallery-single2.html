Instagram Wrapped 

 

Instagram Unwrapped is a minimalistic website, with carousel images designed in Figma and the demo code with HTML bootstrap templates to showcase data surrounding the common behavior of internet stalking.

The Stalking Edition of Instagram 

Our mutual observations about internet stalking and the popularity of Spotify Wrapped inspired this project. We originally planned to create a browser extension that provided commentary on a user’s decision to view an Instagram profile based on the habits it recognizes. However, we realized that we wanted to examine other behaviors as well. Spotify had just released its annual user statistics, and we noticed how everyone rushed to post their final numbers. Thus, we created an aesthetically pleasing site to display and summarize a year’s worth of stalking data to Instagram users and receive an analysis of whether people would share any information about their actions about something that is usually regarded as mortifying if known by the general audience. Moreover, the project is an explicit reflection of data collection that most companies have about our engagement with certain pages and people to customize options such as the ‘for you’ pages. This not only helps them to increase sales on specific products but also increases the time they spend on their page.

In this project, the graphics were kept cohesive by using Instagram’s color palette and the composition style of Spotify Wrapped. Each slide is presented as a slideshow and maintains a semi-judgemental voice, with added icons to convey the tone of each statistic. The comments are there to spark reflection in the user about the magnitude of their behaviors for the past twelve months. This is the current state of our project; however, given more time, we hope to broaden the scope of our site.
As of now, our site’s focus is Instagram, but we would love to look at data from other websites. It could not only be a source of information, but a yearly opportunity for users to see their actions on different social media platforms and decide if they want to bring those habits with them into the following year. Social media addiction is widespread, and this format could act as a starting place for many people trying to fix that problem.

(This project was a group project where the work was eaqually divided. A skeleton template was taken from bootstrap and changes were made for it to resemble the brand identity of Instagram and Spotify)
